About Us

Your trusted partner in premium tiles and granites

Welcome to Shri Selvam Tiles & Granites

Shri Selvam Tiles & Granites is a trusted tiles showroom offering premium quality tiles from leading brands for residential and commercial projects. With over a decade of experience in the tiles industry, we have established ourselves as the go-to destination for homeowners, builders, architects, and interior designers seeking excellence.

Our showroom features an extensive collection of floor tiles, wall tiles, bathroom tiles, kitchen tiles, parking tiles, and cool roof tiles. Each product is carefully selected from renowned manufacturers to ensure superior quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Our Journey

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the tiles industry in our region, Shri Selvam Tiles & Granites began its journey over 10 years ago. What started as a small showroom has now grown into a comprehensive tiles destination, serving thousands of satisfied customers and completing numerous prestigious projects.

Our success story is built on three fundamental pillars: uncompromising quality, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service. We believe that every customer deserves access to premium tiles at affordable prices, backed by expert guidance throughout their selection and installation journey.

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is not just a promise at Shri Selvam Tiles & Granites—it's our identity. We partner exclusively with certified manufacturers and authorized brands to bring you tiles that meet international standards. Every tile in our showroom undergoes rigorous quality checks before it reaches our display.

We understand that tiles are a long-term investment in your property. That's why we stock only those products that we would confidently install in our own homes. From water absorption rates to impact resistance, from color fastness to dimensional accuracy—we verify every parameter.

Customer-First Approach

At the heart of our business is a simple philosophy: your satisfaction is our success. Our experienced team takes the time to understand your specific requirements, budget constraints, and aesthetic preferences. We don't just sell tiles; we help you make informed decisions that you'll be happy with for years to come.

Whether you're renovating a single bathroom or tiling an entire commercial complex, you'll receive the same level of attention, expertise, and service. Our after-sales support ensures that your installation goes smoothly and any concerns are addressed promptly.

Why Homeowners, Builders & Architects Trust Us

For Homeowners: We make your dream home a reality with tiles that combine beauty and functionality. Our consultants help you visualize different options and choose tiles that complement your interior design.

For Builders: We understand project timelines and budget constraints. Our bulk supply capabilities, competitive pricing, and timely delivery make us the preferred choice for construction companies across the region.

For Architects & Designers: Access to the latest trends, premium designer tiles, and custom solutions makes us a valuable partner in bringing your creative visions to life. We stay updated with global tile trends and can source specialty products.

Your Trusted Local Showroom

Unlike online retailers, our physical showroom allows you to see, touch, and compare tiles before making a decision. Walk through our thoughtfully designed display sections, explore different textures and finishes, and get a real sense of how tiles will look in your space.

Our knowledgeable staff is always available to answer your questions, provide samples, and offer professional recommendations. Visit us today and experience the Shri Selvam difference—where quality meets affordability, and customer satisfaction is guaranteed.
